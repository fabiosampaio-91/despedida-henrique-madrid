### Bars — corrections applied
- **Salmon Guru** is in **Huertas/Letras** (doc said Chueca).
- **Inclán Brutal Bar** is in **Barrio de las Letras** (doc said Lavapiés).
- **Josealfredo** is recorded soul/funk/jazz + occasional DJs (**not** nightly live jazz).
- **Bad Company 1920** has **no website** (Instagram-only; that's where the password is posted).

### Clubs — corrections applied
- **Joy Eslava** is now officially **Teatro Eslava** (same building, Arenal 11) — use `teatroeslava.com`.
- **LAB Club** is **LAB theClub inside Chamartín station (north)** and mid-large — **not** a small Chueca club.
- **Fabrik** is in **Humanes (~20 km out)**; `fabrikclub.com` redirects to `fabrikmadrid.com`.
- **Oh My Club** enforces a **25+** door on Sat/Sun.

### Dinner-clubs — corrections / flags
- **Panthera** confirmed (~2.9/5 honest caveat kept). Use the working English page `…/home-english/`.
- **Numa Pompilio** — the "becomes a DJ party" claim is **not** corroborated officially; flagged as the upscale/intimate option.
- **Florida Park** — use `floridapark.es`; the old `floridaretiro.com` is stale/suspect.
- **La Mamona** — booking runs via the Grupo Lalala site.
