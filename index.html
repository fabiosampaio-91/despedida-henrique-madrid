DAY 01

FRIDAY

Arrive · explore · rooftop · bars

  1. 🕐 Lunch

    Land & feast in the centre

    Everyone who's arrived meets for lunch in the city centre. Classic Madrid — a bocadillo de calamares by Plaza Mayor, graze at the market, or a lively Cava Baja taberna.

  2. 🎒 Drop bags

    Hands-free with Bounce

    Don't drag backpacks around all day. Bounce is "Uber for luggage" — leave your bags at a shop/locker around the city and grab them later. (Or just drop them at the accommodation.)

  3. 🚶 Afternoon

    Walk the classics + cañas

    Puerta del Sol → Plaza Mayor → Gran Vía → Malasaña. Soak up the city and stop for cold cañas along the way. No rush — you're warming up.

  4. 🌇 Evening

    Rooftop dinner at sunset

    Dinner up high, drinks as the sun drops, stay as long as it's good. Our Friday pick: Ginkgo Sky Bar — best sunset + a live DJ every Friday. Three top options below 👇

📌 Before we go

📅 Book in advance

Rooftop dinners, the dinner-clubs and the Saturday activity all need reservations. Decide the shortlist, then lock it in — weekends fill up.

👔 Dress codes

Kapital, Barceló & Oh My Club enforce smart/elegant doors (collared shirt, no sneakers/sportswear). Dress sharp or get turned away.

🚌 Fabrik is far

~20 km out. Shuttle coaches leave Callao from ~midnight. If you go, you commit to the night — no quick exit.

🔞 Check the door rules

Oh My Club is 25+ on Sat/Sun. Bad Company 1920 needs the current password from their Instagram. Verify before you trek over.
